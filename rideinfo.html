Ride Information

The Montu


The Montu is an family thrill ride. The Montu takes you to heights of over 140 feet, giving you an excellent view of the whole park. The Montu's top speed is 85km/h.


Minimum height: 48"

Free Fall

While on Free Fall, enjoy the view of the park before you drop over 100 feet.


Minimum height: 46"
